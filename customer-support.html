Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your parcel by going to the Tracking page and entering your 12-digit booking ID. The booking ID is provided to you after completing your booking and payment.

If your parcel is delayed, you can contact our customer support team using the contact information provided on this page. Please have your booking ID ready for reference.

To cancel or modify your booking, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Modifications or cancellations may be subject to terms and conditions.

We currently accept payments through credit cards and debit cards. We plan to add more payment options in the future.
